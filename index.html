About

Murasakino is a Japanese company that specializes in abstract photography and animation. Murasakino creates products according to your vision and what you want to achieve. We provide our services for individuals and companies and do our business worldwide. This company was founded in 2050 by a diverse team of talented and passionate artists. Our team believes that passion turns an "okay" business into a great one.

FAQs

 Where can I see Murasakino's previous projects?

All of the photos on this website have been made by Murasakino. You can also see our previous projects from our photo gallery or YouTube.

 How do I get started?

You can reach us by email or phone or using our contact form. The consultation will be free, and we will gather details on your project. Once we agree on pricing, we will sign an agreement to proceed.

 How much do Murasakino's products cost?

The cost depends on the complexity of the project, but the approximate price ranges from 30€ - 60€ per hour. You can check our pricing table for more info.

 Does Murasakino have any references from past clients?

You can find reviews and references from our clients on our testimonials page. We can also arrange direct references from the people we have worked with.

 When and how do I pay for the project?

We require 50% of the agreed price to start the project and 50% once the project is complete. We make sure you are happy with the results and take the rest of the payment when you approve the project. We accept all major credit cards.
